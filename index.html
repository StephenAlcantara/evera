Mula sa amin · From the founders

Why we built ArugaLife

We started ArugaLife because we kept seeing the same thing: people we love working hard their whole lives, yet feeling unsure about what comes after. In the Philippines, planning for retirement and benefits too often means confusing paperwork, fine print, and figuring it all out alone.

We wanted to build something kinder. A home where care comes first, where guidance is in plain Tagalog or English, and where no Filipino has to face their future by themselves. Evera, made in partnership with the Philippine Retirement Authority, is our first step.

We are six kababayan who believe a secure future should feel less like a transaction and more like coming home. Maraming salamat for being here at the very beginning.
